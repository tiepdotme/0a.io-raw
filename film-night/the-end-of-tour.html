16th February 2019: The End of the Tour (2015)

by Archy Wilhes

A re-enactment of the 1996 road trip interview between Rolling Stone reporter and writer David Lipsky and legendary writer David Foster Wallace best known for his hysterical realism bestseller Infinite Jest published in the same year. The End of the tour (directed by James Ponsoldt) is a fun, subtle and melancholic piece on life paying tribute to David Wallace (who committed suicide in 2008).
